<div class="message">
  <p>
    Summer Smith is an artist and programmer from Lexington, KY.
  </p>
  <p>
    She has a BFA from the Maryland Institute College of Art in classical drawing and a Master of Science from the University of Augusta in Medical Illustration.
  </p>
  <p>
    Her work spans a wide range of techniques and subjects but always shares a love of markmaking and physical media. Using code, she creates generative art that is brought into reality using a pen plotter, a mechanical arm that can be used with various media to "draw" her creations.
  </p>
  <p>
    The code for her generative work can be found on <a href="https://github.com/summasmiff">her Github profile</a>.
  </p>
</div>
